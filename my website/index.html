Living Room

The living room is designed to be a cozy and inviting space for family gatherings and relaxation. It features a comfortable sofa, a stylish coffee table, and a large flat-screen TV mounted on the wall. The room is decorated with modern artwork and soft lighting to create a warm ambiance.
